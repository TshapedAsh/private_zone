About Me

Hi, I’m Gaurav Gawade – a passionate astrophysicist driven by big dreams and a love for human innovation. I believe we have the power to evolve into a Type I civilization by 2100, fully harnessing our planet’s potential. I envision a world by 2050 where human biology is completely in our control – free of diseases, with advanced genetic upgrades and peak mental acuity.

My journey into astrophysics didn’t begin with star-gazing but with the magic of words. Influential books in Marathi by Dr. Jayant Narlikar and inspiring sci-fi shows ignited my passion. Despite the challenges and difficult choices between stability and pursuing research, I remained committed. I’ve completed my master’s in astrophysics and now seek a PhD position with a supervisor who is as dedicated to research as I am.

I also have several innovative research proposals in the works. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to collaborate.
